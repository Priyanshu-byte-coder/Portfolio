About Me

Hey there! I'm Priyanshu Doshi, a B.Tech student specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Nirma University — basically, I teach machines how to think while I'm still figuring out how to get 8 hours of sleep. My curiosity for technology knows no bounds, whether it's exploring how quantum computing could turbocharge AI or breaking down complex neural networks just for fun (yes, that is my idea of fun). When I'm not busy wrestling with code, you'll usually find me on LeetCode battling DSA problems like they owe me money, or experimenting with the latest tech just to see what chaos I can create — from building a 3D robot that follows your cursor, to crafting an unbeatable Tic-Tac-Toe AI (spoiler: you can't win), and even coding a movie recommender system so I never have to ask, "What should I watch tonight?"

Beyond the code, I like to keep things balanced — with a chessboard on one side and a table tennis paddle on the other. I'm also that friend who's always pitching a new startup idea during lunch breaks. Whether it's developing digital platforms to fix real-world problems, creating AI models just for the thrill of it, or typing away on fresh concepts late at night, I love turning ambitious ideas into reality. If you're into AI, tech, or just want to challenge me to a quick chess match (fair warning: I might use AI tricks), feel free to scroll on — there's always more brewing on my side of the screen.
